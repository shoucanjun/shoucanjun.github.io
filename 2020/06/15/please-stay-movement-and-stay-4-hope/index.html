please stay 4 hope

Veröffentlicht am 2020-06-15 23:14:49

Life is struggle,There are some people can’t live a normal life cause of looks,disability,illness,lovesick，family and bully,etc which make they don’t have a normal life.

生活确实很困难，有些人的的生活会因为长相，残疾，病痛，失恋，家人，霸凌，天灾意外等原因让他们没有一个顺利正常的人生。

They must wear a mask if they want to live a normal life but which will make they become weird and depressed

他们迫切的想要跟上正常生活的轨道而必须带着虚假的面具，以至于让他们的性格变得扭曲，变得抑郁。

They are so painful get the way out, someone can get the way out and someone keep alive with drug and autotomy and someone choose to end of their life.

他们很痛苦并非常迫切的想要找到解决办法，有些幸运的找到的解决方法，有些还在靠着药物或自残的方式苟延残喘,有些则选择了结束生命。

Most of people get this disease even me, my family,my classmate ,my friend and my workmate.

这些人不在少数，包括我自己，我认识的家人，同学，朋友，同事都有这些倾向。

You will suffer most of presure in the society today which it will be easy to get this disease.Never keep hiding your painful.you can find The Counselor teacher or tell your friend and family,tell them what you suffer which will make you get the way out.

BTW never keep hiding your disease,it will make you increase your disease gradually.

如今社会生活压力确实很大，绝对不是只有少数人才有的。千万不要隐瞒痛苦，必要时找咨商师，告诉朋友家人，告诉他们你的痛苦，从而走出困境，积压越久病情越重。千万不要有意去隐瞒。

Hope everyone can live a happy life.

希望大家都能过上幸福的生活。
